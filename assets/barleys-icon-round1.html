Embroidery check: on a 3.5″ chest hit, the thinnest element (awn) lands at ≈1.0 mm — right at the satin-stitch floor. A digitizer can bump awns to 1.2 mm without redrawing anything else. Nothing else comes close to the limit.

The one-color versions are what the screen printer and embroiderer will actually ask for. Same geometry, recolored — no separate art to maintain.

What changed vs. 2005
ContainerOval → circle. Avatar frames, app icons, and pins are all circular now; the oval wasted the canvas.
KeylinesThree stacked strokes → one gold ring. The outline-inside-outline construction was the loudest 2005 tell.
IllustrationRedrawn from ~dozens of hairline paths to ~20 flat elements. Detail now lives in negative-space cuts, which fade gracefully instead of turning to mud.
PaletteIcon tier runs 2 inks (red #CB262C / gold #FBBF13). Amber and charcoal rejoin at the lockup tier.
MotifKept. Barley + hop is the equity — same story, same colors, same crossing, one generation newer.
